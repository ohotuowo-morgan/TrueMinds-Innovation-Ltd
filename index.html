Who Are We Looking For

At Trueminds, we believe talent is found in many forms. We welcome applicants
from all educational backgrounds who share our vision for innovation.

Education & Skills

We value competence over certificates. Whether you have a formal degree, are a student intern, or are a self-taught specialist, your ability to deliver results is what matters most.

Practical Experience

We look for a "can-do" attitude. This could be shown through previous jobs, personal projects, open-source contributions, or vocational training relevant to your role.

Reliability & Character

Integrity and teamwork are non-negotiable. We need reliable individuals who communicate clearly and are dedicated to the success of our clients and the community.

Eagerness to Grow

Technology moves fast. We love people who are curious, ask questions, and are excited to pick up new skills on the job to improve themselves and the company.
